Dalia AI Telegram Bot

Perform real-time token analysis and research, combining token statistics, socials, historical and live market data and more with Dalia AI powered conversational Telegram AI bot. Ask it to analyze a token by providing token CA.

Dalia AI Twitter Analyst

Dalia AI powered Twitter analyst provides real-time and automated in-depth analysis of hot/new tokens. The AI bot scans the market and Twitter for newly launched token and provides in-depth analysis of the token and its recommendations right on Twitter.

Dalia AI Recommendation Engine

Dalia AI uses the power of Graph Neural Networks to find and recommend tokens similar to each user's holdings. Discover new and exciting tokens.

Dalia AI Python SDK
